# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Developers, designers, students, terminal users, and other people who spend long sessions at a desktop. They want a lightweight companion that adds warmth and gentle structure without taking focus away from their work.

## Product Purpose

PsyDuck is a persistent pixel-art desktop companion that feels alive through small, intentional reactions. It supports focused work, healthy breaks, and direct interactions while remaining visually secondary to the user's primary applications. Success means the companion is dependable, delightful, resource-conscious, and never disruptive.

## Brand Personality

Curious, friendly, and playful. PsyDuck is quiet, slightly clumsy, and expressive without becoming loud, demanding, or aggressive.

## Anti-references

PsyDuck must not feel like a chatbot, a voice assistant, a notification center, a generic dashboard, or a dense productivity suite. Avoid attention-seeking alerts, decorative motion, noisy controls, non-pixel character treatments, and interfaces that compete with the companion.

## Design Principles

- Keep the character as the visual focus.
- Make every action intentional, subtle, and easy to dismiss.
- Prefer native, familiar interactions over invented controls.
- Preserve pixel consistency while keeping text and controls readable.
- Maintain independent feature boundaries so one interaction never disrupts another.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use clear text contrast, semantic controls, keyboard access where controls are rendered, forced-color compatibility, and reduced-motion behavior. Do not rely on color alone to communicate timer or application state.
