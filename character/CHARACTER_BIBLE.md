# Ducky Character Bible

## Purpose

Ducky is the mascot of the project.

It is a tiny desktop companion that quietly reacts to the user's actions.

The character should immediately feel:

- Cute
- Curious
- Confused
- Friendly
- Soft
- Expressive

It should never feel aggressive, scary, noisy, or distracting.

---

# Style

Retro Game Boy Advance Pokémon inspiration.

Not a direct Pokémon sprite.

Original interpretation inspired by Psyduck.

Should feel like it belongs inside a modern indie game.

---

# Canvas

Master Sprite

256x256

Export Size

64x64

Transparent background

---

# Camera

Front facing

Very slight 3/4 perspective

Never side view for the master sprite.

---

# Proportions

Head

45%

Body

40%

Feet

10%

5%

Small feet.

Short arms.

---

# Outline

Black

2px equivalent

Consistent thickness

---

# Palette

Warm yellow

Light cream beak

Orange shadows

Dark outline

Minimal colors

No gradients

No soft shading

---

# Lighting

Top Left

Single light source

Simple pixel highlights

No bloom

No rim lighting

---

# Personality

Confused

Curious

Sleepy

Playful

Gentle

Friendly

Never angry.

---

# Idle Pose

Standing

Feet apart

Small smile

Relaxed arms

Looking forward

---

# Expression Rules

Eyes carry most emotion.

Body movement is subtle.

Never exaggerate proportions between animations.

---

# Animation Rules

Every animation begins and ends in the Idle pose.

The silhouette must remain recognizable.

Do not redesign the character between animations.

---

# Forbidden

Clothing

Accessories

Background

Environment

Text

Speech bubbles

Props

Complex shadows
